Covid information

covid-19 in recent years has been a global pandemic and it has been a very contagious virus that has taken over 5 million lives globally by causing failures in the respiratory system

symptoms could look like :

  • a high temperature or shivering
  • a new, continuous cough
  • a loss or change to your sense of smell or taste
  • shortness of breath

Most people feel better within a few days or weeks of their first COVID-19 symptoms and make a full recovery within 12 weeks.
